How It Works

1. Capture

Webcam captures video frames in real-time. All processing is local — no images leave your device.

2. Pose Estimation

MediaPipe detects 33 body landmarks in 2D, then VideoPose3D lifts them to 3D coordinates.

3. GCN Analysis

A Spatio-Temporal Graph Convolutional Network analyzes the 3D skeleton across 30 temporal frames.

4. Classification

Classifies posture as Normal or FHP with confidence scores and biomechanical analysis.
